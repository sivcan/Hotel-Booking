We are Family. Our Resort is for a family friendly holiday

A family friendly resort that welcomes both big and small. With two separate pool area of which one comprises of three shallower pools, a bakery and a Kids Club, we hope that families will feel right at home at Resorts & Spa. In addition to regular babysitting – “yaya” – services for the children of guests, we have Kids’ Cove, a kid’s club on premises with access to fully equipped play rooms, outdoor playground and indoor classrooms with educational material and toys. For the older kids we offer PADI Bubble Maker Dive Courses, the PADI Seal Team program as well as snorkelling programs and private swimming lessons. We also offer cater to family in the spa with Kid’s yoga and a Kid’s Spa Menu.
